Season 1 · 2022

Break the Silence

7 guests · The first brave step

Season 1 didn't try to solve anything. It tried to make space. Fonterra people told stories they'd never said out loud at work — loss, depression, the weight of carrying it alone. The bravery isn't in the answers; it's in the fact that anyone showed up to answer at all.

Season 2

Identity & Rebuilding

5 guests · From disclosure to reconstruction

If Season 1 was about breaking silence, Season 2 was about what you do next. Guests stopped asking whether they could talk and started asking who they wanted to become afterwards. The stories got more specific, the pain more named, and the hope more earned.

Season 3

Tools & Prevention

6 guests · From story to practice

Season 3 pivoted. Instead of "here's what happened to me," guests brought something closer to "here's what actually helps." Financial wellbeing, physical health, stoic thinking, self-kindness. The podcast stopped being only a confessional and started being a toolkit.

Season 4

Leadership as Practice

6 guests · The mirror turns to leaders

By Season 4, the conversation matured into something harder. It wasn't "be a better leader" — it was "the way you show up is already teaching your team what's allowed." Silence as a leadership act. Role-modelling as the real policy. The bravest move yet: asking leaders to see themselves.
